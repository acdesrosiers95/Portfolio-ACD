About me

After high school I went to college to be a diesel mechanic at Universal Technical Institue in Norwood, Massachusetts. I graduated college in 2015 and have been working in my field sense. Im currently running the shop as the shop foreman at John Deere in Plainfield, Conecticut. I became a diesel mechanic because im a huge fan of puzzles and thats they way I view working on trucks and equipment. I enjoy picking things apart and figureing out what makes them tick.
